Home-based palliative care is a specialized medical service that provides comprehensive support to individuals with serious, life-limiting illnesses within the comfort of their own homes. This approach focuses on enhancing quality of life by addressing physical symptoms, as well as emotional, social, and spiritual needs. It involves a multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, social workers, and counselors, who collaborate to deliver personalized care tailored to the patient's and family's preferences.
